1. The Problem
Health inequalities remain a significant challenge in public health, with variations in disease outcomes observed across different regions and demographic groups. Understanding how infectious disease trends differ by factors such as geography and age is essential for effective public health planning and intervention.
This project investigates how infectious disease metrics vary across the UK, with a focus on identifying potential inequalities in trends over time.


2. The Objective
Analyse infectious disease trends over time
Compare patterns across regions and demographic groups
Identify potential health inequalities
Generate insights to support public health decision-making
Present findings through an interactive dashboard
